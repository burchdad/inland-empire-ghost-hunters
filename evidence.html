Submit a Case Request Email Evidence

Include these details

  • Date and time of the evidence
  • Location or room where it happened
  • What you noticed
  • Whether anyone else was present
  • Possible normal explanations, including pets, cars, wind, plumbing, neighbors, insects, dust, reflections, or electronics

Review Method

Evidence is treated as case material, not entertainment.

Original files help

Original media is more useful than reposted or heavily compressed clips when possible.

Timelines matter

Written timelines help compare reported activity with camera footage, audio, and environmental notes.

Permission matters

Evidence is not published to YouTube without permission from the client or property owner.
