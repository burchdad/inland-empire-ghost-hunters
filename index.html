Our Approach

Careful observation and private evidence review

Our investigations are based on careful observation, controlled documentation, and private evidence review. We do not claim every sound, shadow, or movement is paranormal.

Your privacy matters. Names, addresses, photos, videos, and case details are kept confidential. We do not publish evidence, location information, or private details without permission.

Submitting evidence

For now, please email photos, videos, audio/EVP recordings, security clips, or written timelines to evidence@inlandempireghosthunters.com.

  • Date and time of the evidence
  • Location or room where it happened
  • What you noticed
  • Whether anyone else was present
  • Possible normal explanations, including pets, cars, wind, plumbing, neighbors, insects, dust, reflections, or electronics
