Inland Empire Ghost Hunters

A serious local team for sensitive unexplained activity.

Inland Empire Ghost Hunters provides respectful and confidential paranormal investigations throughout the Inland Empire. Our approach focuses on documentation, not drama. We use live multi-camera video monitoring, EVP/audio review, and environmental equipment to help document reported activity as clearly as possible.

Live Multi-Camera Video Monitoring

Inland Empire Ghost Hunters uses fixed camera coverage that allows selected areas to be viewed live during an investigation while also recording footage for later review.

Our Approach

Careful observation and private evidence review

Our investigations are based on careful observation, controlled documentation, and private evidence review. We do not claim every sound, shadow, or movement is paranormal.
